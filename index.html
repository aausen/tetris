Welcome to Tetris!

Let's Play Tetris

Tetris is a classic puzzle arcade game where the player tries to stack as many tetrominos as they can before it reaches the top.

Tetrominos decend from the top of the screen and players are able to rotate and move the tetrominos to best stack them together. When an entire row is filled in, the row is taken out and the player recieves points. Get 150 points and you win! Stack them up all way to the top and your game is over. See how many points you can score before your game is over!
